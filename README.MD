VWAP STRATERGY:

The VWAP strategy is an intraday options buying strategy focused on the NIFTY market. Strike selection is based on the current-month NIFTY futures chart on a 1-minute timeframe, where the close of the first 1-minute candle after market open is taken as ATM; from this, the CE strike is selected as 2 ITM and the PE strike as 2 ITM. After selecting the strikes, each option (CE and PE) is traded independently on its own 1-minute option chart using the VWAP indicator. From 9:16 onwards, if any candle closes above the VWAP line, a buy entry of 1 lot is taken on the next candle, and the position is held as long as price remains above VWAP. If any candle closes below the VWAP, the open position is exited on the next candle. In addition to individual exits, a cumulative MTM risk rule is applied: on every candle, the combined MTM of all running trades is calculated, and if the total profit or loss reaches ±3000, all positions are force-exited immediately and no further trades are allowed for that trading day.
